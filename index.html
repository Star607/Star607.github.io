Biography

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University and a member of VIPA Group, supervised by Prof. Mingli Song. I am fortunate to collaborate with Assistant Professor Yuxuan Liang from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). In 2024, I was selected for the Doctoral Student Special Program of Youth Talent Project from China Association for Science and Technology (CAST).

My research focuses on AI for science, particularly spatiotemporal data modeling using graph learning techniques to address critical challenges in urban science. Key applications include urban planning, disaster management and epidemic prevention, leveraging urban mobility data to enhance data-driven decision-making.

Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research :)
